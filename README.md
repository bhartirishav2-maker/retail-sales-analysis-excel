# 📊 Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes a retail dataset to uncover insights into sales performance and profitability.

## 🎯 Business Problem

Despite strong sales, the business is facing low or negative profit in certain categories due to discounting and cost inefficiencies.

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

![Dashboard](Screenshot%20\(29\).png)

## 🔍 Key Insights

* High sales does not always lead to high profit
* Furniture category shows consistent losses
* Excessive discounting impacts profitability
* Profit varies across regions and product segments

## 🚀 Recommendations

* Limit discounting on low-margin products
* Focus on high-profit categories
* Reprice or remove loss-making products
* Optimize logistics and operational costs

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Dashboarding)

## 📁 Dataset

Publicly available Superstore dataset (for learning purposes only)
